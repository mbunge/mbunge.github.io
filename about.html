I ride the wave of curiosity in an almost endless ocean of ideas. The never-ending sunrise excites me. I am determined to set sail towards the eternally rising sun.

Hello! I'm an avid Magic: The Gathering enthusiast who began my journey in the world of strategic card play back in 2003, starting with the Legacy format. My passion for gaming extends into the realm of tabletop role-playing games (TTRPGs), particularly Dungeons & Dragons. Currently, I'm the Dungeon Master for two different gaming tables and am in the process of authoring an exciting, upcoming adventure.

Away from the gaming table, I cherish spending quality time with my wife and dog. Music is another of my pursuits; I enjoy playing the guitar. Additionally, I find great pleasure in cooking, always eager to try out new recipes and create delicious dishes.
